The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Drawn Together Through A Sordid Mistake?

Zende and Poppy could be drawn together through a sordid mistake-the mistake that happened when Luna Nozawa (Lisa Yamada) accidentally took some of her mother’s “special mints”.

Mixed with a small amount of alcohol, the narcotic substances in the mints made Luna high and made her hallucinate Zende was RJ Forrester (Joshua Hoffman).

Luna would never have had sex with Zende otherwise, she’s in love with RJ-she’s yet to confess it to him but seems resigned to live with her guilt.

Luna, Zende and Poppy had quite a discussion about what had happened and Luna lashed out at her mother over the mints and blamed her for the issue. However, Zende and Poppy made a connection over Luna’s and Zende’s problem, and it’s obvious Zende’s worried about Luna’s pain.

B & B Spoilers: Mother And Daughter In The Same Boat

When it all comes down to it, mother and daughter are in the same boat because they both popped those narcotic-laced mints. It’s a good possibility they may be a little stronger than merely “edibles” laced with THD, a derivative of marijuana.

They’re keeping the secret of what happened that night between Zende and Luna because of those mints, along with Luna. Zende would love to be involved romantically in a more real with Luna, but it’s obvious that’s not going to happen.

Zende won’t ever get that wish, but perhaps something else might happen-maybe he and Poppy might have a night or two of passion?

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Zende Forrester-Dominguez Finds Himself In Need Of Comfort

Zende could easily find himself in need of comfort, and Poppy may as well-Zende touched Poppy’s arms in a kind, but suggestive way in a recent B & B episode.

He had praised Poppy for how well Luna had turned out and he had tried to comfort her. It’s possible the groundwork could be laid for Zende to take a second Nozawa woman to bed, bringing with it many, many complications!

Zende and Poppy could well find sparks igniting between them, and the sparks lead to a night of passion in spite of Poppy casually seeing Dollar Bill. But what would happen if they were caught in the act, by either Bill or maybe by Luna-that could be dramatic!
