Diddy's Sexed-Up Fragrance Ad With Cassie Is NSFW and &quot;Too Hot for TV&quot;

If sex sells, Diddy‘s new fragrance is going to fly off the shelves faster than you can say “NSFW.” The mogul, 45, teamed up with longtime girlfriend Cassie, 28, in a steamy, sexed-up ad for his latest scent, 3AM — and let’s just say the words “too hot to handle” have never been more apropos. 

The one-minute ad — which is, truly, NSFW — features a tuxedo-clad Sean “Diddy” Combs getting it on (very graphically at points) with his longtime love, who appears in various states of undress throughout the video. At one point, she appears completely naked while straddling him on a little patch of fake green grass; at another, she flashes a hint of what looks like a nipple ring.

Toward the end, the two go all-in and simulate sex in bed. It’s explicit, to say the least — so much so that the campaign was banned from network TV, according to Page Six. 

“ATTENTION!!! I’m debuting my NEW #3AMseanjohn campaign TODAY!” the rapper tweeted. “They say it’s TOO HOT for TV!”

Speaking about the ad with Style.com, which premiered the clip on Monday, May 4, Diddy said it represents what 3 a.m. is for him.
