Kanye West Seemingly Replaces Jay-Zs Donda Verse with DaBaby, Leaving Fans Outraged

February 03, 2024
blog

Kanye West is sparking outrage on social media due to multiple things that happened during his latest Donda listening event.

The 44-year-old rapper hosted the third listening event at Chicago’s Soldier Field and it was streamed on Apple Music yet again.

Two controversial figures made appearances at the event and we learned that a beloved rapper has seemingly been removed from the album.

Click inside to learn about what happened…

DaBaby and Marilyn Manson both made appearances during the event.

For those who don’t know, DaBaby recently caused controversy when he made homophobic, misogynistic and ignorant comments at the Rolling Loud Festival. Marilyn was recently accused of abuse by many women, including his ex-fiance Evan Rachel Wood.

Jay-Z previously provided a verse for a song on Donda and it was played publicly for the first time during the first listening event on July 22. We even learned some cool new details about how the verse was made!

At the third listening party, fans learned that Jay-Z‘s verse has seemingly been replaced by one recorded by DaBaby.

Manson made an appearance at the listening party and was seen hanging on the stairs of the house that was constructed on stage.
