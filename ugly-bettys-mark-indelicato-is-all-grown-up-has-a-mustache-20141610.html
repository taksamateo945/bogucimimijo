Ugly Betty's Mark Indelicato Is All Grown Up, Has a Mustache

January 23, 2024
blog

Justin Suarez, is that you? Ugly Betty's Mark Indelicato — who played the title character's vivacious, fashion-savvy nephew on the series — looked all grown up when he hit the red carpet on Wednesday, Oct. 15, for a New York screening of his new movie, White Bird in a Blizzard, costarring Shailene Woodley. 

The now-20-year-old actor sported a mustache and goatee, and wore his dark hair slicked back in a mini man bun. His outfit was head-to-toe black — black T-shirt, black blazer, black pants, and black platform-soled shoes.

Indelicato has kept a relatively low on-screen profile since Ugly Betty went off the air in 2010, but he's been keeping busy with other things. According to his Twitter account, he's a student at NYU, as well as a fashion blogger who writes under the moniker "The Fashion Gangster." White Bird in a Blizzard is his first major feature film.

Back in 2012, the actor did an interview for NYU Local about what it was like to make the transition from TV star to student. "I'm not the type of person that looks for people to recognize me and talk to me," he said at the time, adding later that he's really "shy and awkward" in person. 

"On the first day of classes…I was like nauseous. I didn't want to go," he admitted. "I feel like the way that I dress…I don't really blend in with the crowd normally, so people mistake that as me being confident, when in reality, I am so, so shy."
