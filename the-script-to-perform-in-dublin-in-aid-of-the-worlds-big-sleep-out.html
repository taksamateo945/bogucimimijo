The Script to perform in Dublin in aid of The Worlds Big Sleep Out

June 21, 2024
blog

The Script have announced they are to perform in Dublin on December 7th as part of The Worlds Big Sleep Out.

The band will perform at Trinity College as part of the line up for The Worlds Big Sleep Out,  the event has been thought up by Josh Littlejohn MBE, and Alice Thompson, who was recently recognised for all the work they’ve done to end homelessness, at The Pride of Britain Awards.

Events will also take place in over 50 locations around the world with the aim of raising $50 million to fight global homelessness.

In 2017, The The Last Time singers,- who release their latest album Sunsets & Full Moons on November 8th, -supported a charity,  A Sense of Home, a community-based solution for creating homes for aged-out foster youth. The model is to be replicated across the USA and the Western World, with their video for Arms Open.

Other acts included in concerts across the world include Tom Walker, Randy Jackson, Will Smith, Travis and Frank Turner.

Registration is now open for people to sign up and host their own event, in their backyard, school, office etc. Each participant committing to fundraising as much as they can – with 100 per cent of funds raised set to go towards homelessness charities, with a 50/50 split between local and global charities helping individuals who are homeless or have no fixed address.
