Demi Lovato Is 6 Months Sober After Scary Overdose Check Out Her Best Day Ever!

March 29, 2024
blog

The singer went through the scariest experience of her life when she suffered a drug overdose and nearly passed away! But now, she can proudly say that she has been sober for no less than six months and she has no intention of going back!

Demi Lovato took to social media to document her milestone since, as it seems, her team surprised her with cake and more!

There was also a card that read: ‘Happy 6. Mo. We are so f***ing proud of you!’

Demi posted the pic on her Instagram Stories and made sure to mention that it was ‘the best day ever!’

Source: instagram.com

And the celebration did not end there! The former Disney star also shared a snap of her holding her sobriety coin that read ‘Six Months‘ and captioned it with three prayer hand emojis.

After taking a social media break while getting help in rehab, Demi Lovato has started to open up about her overdose again.

Just last month, she posted on her platform of choice, Instagram, that she was ‘grateful for the lessons [she’s] learned this year.’

‘I will never take another day in life for granted, even the bad ones. Thankful for my fans, friends, family, and everyone who supported me through this year. God bless,’ she went on to write in a story.

A few days before, she had asked her fans for understanding and privacy.
